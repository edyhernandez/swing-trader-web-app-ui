Welcome to SWINGTrader!

SwingTrader applies the rules of IBD's CAN SLIM Investing System in a Swing Trading environment to help you take advantage of short term trends.

ABOUT SWINGTrader

Unique Approach

Unlike other swing trade products SwingTrader combines fundamental analysis with technical analysis to increase your potential for success.

Simple to Use

You can scan the list in a couple of minutes to determine if action is required. Other swing trade products can be overly complex, making you develop the criteria, run the screens, find the stocks and determine the setups. SwingTrader does it for you.

For each trade you get:

  • Trade Setup
  • Buy Zone
  • Profit Goal
  • Stop Loss

Strategy

We go for quick hits. Most trades will have a goal of about 10% on the upside and limit risk on the downside with a stop loss around 3%.

Alerts to Keep You Current

With your subscription, you'll receive Swing Trader provides email alerts and push notifications to keep you up to speed on the action.

The SWINGTrader Team

Our team is focused on one thing: To help you make more money in the stock market!

The Swing Trader Team is comprised of market experts that have more than 50 years combined experience with the CAN SLIM Investing System and performing detailed market analysis. They use multiple screens with key fundamental and technical criteria and other crucial factors to come up with swing trade ideas.
